The 4-Day Output Cycle
🗣️ Speak Day
30 minutes of free talk — explain your day, argue both sides of a topic, describe something you read. No scripts. You produce from your own head.
✍️ Write Day
30 minutes of writing — casual journal, a short opinion paragraph, or a Twitter-style thread. Saved in your journal so you can see progress over time.
⚡ React Day
Read or watch something for 10–15 min (article, YouTube, Reddit). Then spend the rest responding in your own words — agree/disagree, summary, reaction. Input is just the trigger.
✨ Polish Day
Revisit old journal entries. Rewrite awkward sentences. Expand short paragraphs. Notice your own patterns and fix them. This is where the real improvement compounds.
🔥 Streak Rules
Skipping 1–2 days does not reset your streak. Only 3+ consecutive missed days resets it. Target is 80% consistency — not perfection.
