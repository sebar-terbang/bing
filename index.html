How It Works
🔥 Streak Rules
Skipping 1–2 days does not reset your streak. Only 3+ consecutive missed days will reset it. The goal is 80% consistency — not perfection.
⏱ Daily Routine — 30 min
10 min Anki vocabulary · 10 min reading an article · 10 min listening on YouTube. Plus an optional writing session to strengthen your output.
📈 Progress Mindset
Small daily actions compound over months. Even on busy days, completing just one session keeps the habit alive and the streak safe.
